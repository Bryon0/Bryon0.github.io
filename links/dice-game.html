Rules of the game

This is a turn based game, in which each round a player has up to three rolls per turn. Each roll, the player can set aside any number of dice and roll the remaining in order to get a certain combination of numbers. After the last roll you will have to add up the maximum potential points that are still available to score.

Ones Total of Ones only
Twos Total of Twos only
Threes Total of Threes only
Fours Total of Fours only
Fives Total of Fives only
Sixes Total of Sixes only
3 of a Kind Total of all 5 dice
4 of a Kind Total of all 5 dice
Full House 25 Points
Small Straight 30 Points
Large Straight 40 Points
YAHTZEE 50 Points
